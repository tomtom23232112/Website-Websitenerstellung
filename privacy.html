Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

EverAdam ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit everadam.com or any related subdomain, or engage our website-mockup and hosting services, how we use that information, and the choices you have. By using this website, you consent to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect three categories of information:

  • Information you provide directly — when you submit a form on this site, we collect your name, email address, current website URL (if any), and the industry you selected.
  • Information collected automatically — when you visit our site, our analytics tools may collect your IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, UTM parameters from ad campaigns, pages viewed, time on site, and approximate location (country/region level). We use cookies and similar technologies to do this.
  • Billing information — if you become an active subscriber, we collect billing data (name, billing address, payment method) through our third-party payment processor. We do not store full credit-card numbers on our own servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver the free Mockup you requested.
  • To send you transactional emails related to your project, subscription, and invoices.
  • To process recurring monthly payments through our payment processor.
  • To measure and improve the performance of our advertising and this website.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, spam, or misuse of our services.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

We rely on the following third parties, which may receive information from your visit:

  • Google Analytics 4 — for site analytics. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google Ads — for conversion measurement and remarketing.
  • Email and CRM providers — to store and respond to leads and client communications.
  • Payment processor — to bill recurring monthly subscriptions. Card data is handled directly by the processor under PCI-DSS standards.

4. Cookies

We use first-party cookies to remember your session and third-party cookies (Google Analytics, Google Ads) for analytics and advertising. You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some features of this site work.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct or delete it.
  • Opt out of marketing communications (use the unsubscribe link in any email we send).
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ads Settings page.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at contact@everadam.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain lead and project information for as long as needed to deliver our service and comply with our legal obligations. For active subscribers, we retain billing records for the period required by applicable tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years). You may request deletion of marketing data at any time; we retain only the minimum required for legal compliance.

7. Security

We use industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

8. Children

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed to anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised. Material changes will be communicated on this page.

10. Contact Us
